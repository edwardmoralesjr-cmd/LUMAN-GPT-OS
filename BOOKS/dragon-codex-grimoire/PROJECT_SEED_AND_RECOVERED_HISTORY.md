# The Dragon Codex Grimoire — Project Seed and Recovered History

## State

Roseborn companion book with a strong established concept.

## Core Purpose

Create a full illustrated grimoire containing the twelve dragons from the prehistory of the Roseborn Saga, plus the Petal Yet Unseen / Living Flame.

## Core Entries

1. Elarion — The First Flame
2. Myrrak — The Hollow Mirror
3. Aurelith — The Spiral of Time
4. Na'shara — The Root of Memory
5. Drak'hym — The Resonant Word
6. Velune — The Mirror of Tides
7. Sorynth — The Stone of Endurance
8. Kaevor — The Gate of Release
9. Ysilra — The Wing of Vision
10. Thalvyn — The Sacred Balance
11. Zarynth — The Unbound Flame
12. Ishkala — The Infinite Bloom
13. The Petal Yet Unseen — The Living Flame

## Recovered Entry Components

Each dragon may include:

- Name and title
- Symbol
- Glyph
- Crest
- Sacred poem
- Domain
- Doctrine
- Virtues
- Rites
- Taboos
- Sacred spaces
- Present-age distortions
- Mythic biography
- Relationship to Codices, Petals, houses, and historical eras

## Dragon Doctrine Summary

- Elarion: sacred calling and noble ignition
- Myrrak: truth through rupture, with nihilistic distortion
- Aurelith: cyclical time and disciplined timing
- Na'shara: memory, ancestry, and lineage
- Drak'hym: sacred speech, naming, and vow
- Velune: grief, emotional truth, and healing
- Sorynth: endurance joined to purpose
- Kaevor: release and holy endings
- Ysilra: elevated vision and right scale
- Thalvyn: balance under pressure
- Zarynth: liberation from false bonds
- Ishkala: wholeness without erasing difference
- Living Flame: future emergence beyond the current order
