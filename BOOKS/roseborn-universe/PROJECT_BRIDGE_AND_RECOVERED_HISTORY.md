## Core Identity

The Roseborn Universe is a mythic fantasy and spiritual cosmology built around:

- The Infinite Bloom
- The seven creation mechanics of the Architect Layer
- The Codices
- The Codex Fracture
- Twelve dragons and the Petal Yet Unseen
- Petals and Ascendants
- Auracites
- Sacred houses, glyphs, sigils, crests, and spiral marks
- Chronosteel
- The Choir of the Unshaped
- Mortal Primacy
- The Third Emergence
- Grand Generals as the grounded reader entry point

## Foundational Cosmology

### The Infinite Bloom

The primal source-state before fracture and differentiation.

### Architect Layer

1. Pulse
2. Lattice of Becoming
3. Echo Descent
4. Codex Reflection
5. Fractal Evolution
6. Sovereign Principle
7. Mortal Primacy

### Codex Fracture

The defining catastrophe. Myrrak is tied to betrayal and the fracture current.

### Codices

Sacred repositories of truth, memory, power, transformation, and hidden cosmological order.

### Seventh Codex

A sentient creator-being disguised as a tangible entity, with autobiographical resonance to Edward's life.

## Dragon Structure

- Elarion — sacred calling and noble ignition
- Myrrak — truth through rupture, distorted toward nihilism
- Aurelith — cyclical time and disciplined timing
- Na'shara — remembrance and lineage
- Drak'hym — sacred speech, naming, and vow
- Velune — emotional truth, grief, and reflective healing
- Sorynth — endurance joined to purpose
- Kaevor — release and holy endings
- Ysilra — elevated vision and scale
- Thalvyn — balance as right relation under pressure
- Zarynth — liberation from false bonds
- Ishkala — radiant wholeness without erasing difference
- Petal Yet Unseen / Living Flame — future emergence beyond the twelvefold order
