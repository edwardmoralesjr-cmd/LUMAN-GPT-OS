# Internal Structural Conflict

The document names Movement II a `Decalogy`, which means a ten-book sequence, but assigns twelve books to it.

Arithmetic:

```text
2 + 12 + 3 + 5 + 1 = 23
```

The movement labels themselves imply:

```text
2 + 10 + 3 + 5 + 1 = 21
```

The two titles added beyond the true ten-book Decalogy were:

```text
The Academy of Petals
The Last Bloom Before Silence
```

# Preservation Decision

The 23-book version is not erased. It is retained as an expanded development variant.

Its two overflow titles are now Reserved Expansion Material under the active 21-book architecture.
