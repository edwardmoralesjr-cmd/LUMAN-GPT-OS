## What Is Established

The April 2026 Roseborn Universe Master Story Bible states that earlier Roseborn planning included an `official 20-book blueprint organized across five movements`.

The same source says later planning expanded and refined the architecture and that the counts needed future reconciliation without losing the core movements.

The April 2026 rolling continuity also identifies the 20-book blueprint as recovered prior canon competing with the then-current 23-book structure.

## What Is Not Established

The accessible source files do not preserve:

- The complete 20-title list
- Exact movement book counts
- Book numbering
- Which title or titles differed from later versions
- Whether Grand Generals had the same five-book placement
- Whether the 20-book count was arithmetic, public-facing, or an incomplete draft

## Non-Invention Rule
