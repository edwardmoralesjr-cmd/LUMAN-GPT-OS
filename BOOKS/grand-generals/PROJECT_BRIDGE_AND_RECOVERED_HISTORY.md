## The Listening Chamber

An ancient resonance-testing threshold space that responds to presence, pattern, and alignment rather than brute force.

Features:

- Worn stone passages
- Spiral or petal geometry
- Partially erased glyphwork
- Acoustically strange spaces
- Footsteps, breath, and voice behaving unnaturally

Auron receives the clearest response. Liora senses relational and emotional residue. Solren recognizes the threshold and presses forward.

## The Buried Heart

The deepest known sacred center beneath Cairnwake.

- Fractured symmetry
- Old resonance lines
- Sealed access logic
- Partial Bloom geometry damaged by the Fracture
- Recognition point tied to memory, seal law, and dormant city response

Book One climax site where truth, suppression, and ancient recognition collide.

## Antagonist Frame

The antagonist is the face of suppression, descended from a fractured protective order.

Core belief:


They preserve managed forgetting not from cartoon evil, but from fear that awakening the buried truth will reopen catastrophe.

Sevran Vale is associated with the final Seal Warden confrontation in the Buried Heart.
