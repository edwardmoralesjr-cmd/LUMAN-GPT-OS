## Recovered Style Direction

- Epic and morally serious fantasy
- Psychological and supernatural horror
- Fast thriller pacing and compact chapter momentum

The influence references used in conversation included Terry Goodkind, Stephen King, and James Patterson. The final work should develop its own voice rather than imitate any living or deceased author too closely.

## Core Format

- Serialized chapters
- Strong opening hooks
- Escalating mystery
- Short or tightly paced installments
- Chapter-ending turns
- Dark fantasy atmosphere
- Psychological stakes
- A larger hidden truth gradually revealed
