# The Seven-Codex Sequence

## Program Identity

The Rose Codices are a connected spiritual, philosophical, poetic, and mythic library rather than unrelated books.

## Working Sequence

```text
[1] The Infinite Bloom — source-state / original unity
[2] The Black Rose Codex — the Tear
[3] The Red Rose Codex — the Pulse
[4] The Yellow Rose Codex — the Tenderness
[5] The Voidborn Codex — the Return to Zero
[6] The White Rose Codex — the Gathering
[7] The Seventh Codex — the Hidden Smile / creator key
```

## Recovered Program Purpose

- Translate esoteric wisdom into emotionally accessible language.
- Transform pain into doctrine, poetry, self-understanding, and creative meaning.
- Explore fracture, love, self-love, surrender, integration, and lived divinity.
- Serve as both spiritual texts and foundations beneath the Roseborn Universe.
- Preserve the possibility that the Seventh Codex is simultaneously a sentient creator-being in fiction and an autobiographical revelation in nonfiction.

## Relationship to Roseborn

The Codices exist in two related modes:

1. Real-world spiritual and philosophical books written by Edward.
2. Sacred repositories of truth, memory, power, and transformation inside the Roseborn cosmology.

These modes may mirror one another, but project files must distinguish published doctrine, working spiritual material, and fictional canon.

## Relationship to Lucid Syntax

The Codices, Infinite Bloom, and Roseborn provide a deep mythic and spiritual source for future Lucid Syntax albums, particularly the planned `Infinite Bloom` source-album.

## Governance Rule

No draft or remembered concept becomes published doctrine or Locked Roseborn Canon without explicit Edward approval.
