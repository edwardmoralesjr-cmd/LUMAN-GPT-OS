## Production Preferences

- Clean, scalable line art
- Strong edge clarity
- Print-safe margins
- Black-and-white interiors
- High-resolution print-ready PDF
- Intentional page balance
- Avoid overly muddy or gray line work
- Preserve symbolic accuracy when using Roseborn or Codex imagery
