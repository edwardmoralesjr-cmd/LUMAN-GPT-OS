# Top Comment Terror Anthology — Project Seed and Recovered History

## State

Concept derived from Edward's interactive horror-thriller channel and series format.

## Original Channel Identity

```text
Top Comment Terror
```

Interactive horror in which the best or top comment after a set window determines the next episode's direction.

## Core Engagement Structure

- Short horror-thriller episode
- Strong final dilemma
- Viewers decide what happens next
- Audience explains why
- Next episode follows the selected path
- Call to action directs viewers to the profile for more series

## Known Episode Seed

### Dead Air

- Time: 3:33 AM
- A radio warns the protagonist not to turn around
- A voicemail arrives from tomorrow
- The story invokes the comments as part of the unfolding threat

## Book Adaptation Possibilities

### Branching Horror Novel

Readers choose among paths at the end of chapters.

### Anthology of Audience-Built Stories

Collect completed interactive arcs, including alternate paths rejected by the audience.

### Experimental Meta-Horror

The comments, readers, or voting audience become part of the story's supernatural mechanism.

### Creator's Edition

Include the original prompt, winning audience decision, final episode, and alternate endings.
