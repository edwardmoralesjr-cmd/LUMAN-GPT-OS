# Run Your Own Code - Expanded Framework

## Status

Recovered source snapshot. This file preserves the structure and core claims of the original expanded framework that preceded *The Immediate Field*. It is provenance material, not the current governing manuscript.

## Original Function

The framework proposed a practical model for conscious living in which human beings learn to recognize the biological, psychological, cultural, spiritual, and institutional forces shaping perception and behavior, then develop a deliberate internal operating process.

## Core Architecture

### Architecture of Consciousness

- Survival hardware: threat detection, belonging, status, pleasure seeking, energy conservation, pattern recognition, and predictive response.
- Narrative software: family stories, cultural norms, religious teachings, political identities, economic expectations, personal memory, and self-concept.
- Divided-mind archetypes: comparative traditions describing internal conflict, intrusive or inherited thought, temptation, ego, illusion, and fragmentation.
- Directional Engine: a practical process for observing, regulating, testing, choosing, acting, reviewing, and refining.

### Architecture of Civilization

- Civilizations depend on shared stories, legitimacy, trust, authority, ritual, memory, and institutional continuity.
- Institutions survive when they can adapt outer form while protecting useful inner purpose.
- The Templar and Hospitaller examples were used as historical analogies, with the Hospitaller model becoming the basis of an adaptability protocol.

### Narrative as Technology of Control

Narratives shape behavior through repeated exposure, emotional activation, identity attachment, social reinforcement, and behavioral command. Power operates partly by determining which stories receive attention and which emotional states accompany them.

### Religion as Architecture of Trust

Religion was explored as one historical container for trust, meaning, ethical instruction, identity, ritual, community, and transmission. The framework does not reduce religion to control and does not treat distinct traditions as identical.

### Micro-Narrative of Self-Love

The working Core Directive emerged as:

> I am already worthy, and from that worthiness I will build.

Self-love was defined as non-contingent worth joined to responsibility, discipline, relationship, and action rather than indulgence or exemption.

## Integrated Practices

- Sacred Pause
- Emotional Firewall
- Fracture Mapping
- Hospitaller Protocol
- Immediate Field Check
- Six-command daily loop: Filter, Regulate, Separate, Test, Choose, Iterate
- Ninety-second re-entry practice
- Weekly review and physical-outcome testing

## Original Synthesis Thesis

The mind runs on story. Power competes to shape story. Freedom begins when consciousness learns to observe, test, revise, and responsibly author the narratives guiding physical life.

## Relationship to The Immediate Field

*The Immediate Field* is the parent-book evolution of this source. It keeps `Run Your Own Code` as a central method while expanding the philosophy into body, attention, environment, relationship, family, repair, storytelling, work, creativity, and the next verifiable action.
