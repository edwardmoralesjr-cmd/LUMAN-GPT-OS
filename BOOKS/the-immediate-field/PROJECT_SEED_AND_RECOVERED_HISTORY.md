## Origin

The project began as `Run Your Own Code - A Framework for Conscious Living`, a research-informed framework connecting survival hardware, narrative software, civilization, religion and trust, self-love, the Directional Engine, the Sacred Pause, the Immediate Field, and embodied practice.

A later relational expansion established that sovereignty is incomplete when practiced only in solitude. Family, repair, storytelling, caregiving, home rhythms, creative partnership, and co-regulation became central rather than secondary.

On 2026-07-14, Edward selected the title `The Immediate Field` and approved the subtitle `Reclaiming Agency in the Body, the Home, the Family, and the Next Action`.

## Major Structural Decision

`The Immediate Field` became the parent book.

`Run Your Own Code` remains a central internal method and planned chapter.

The book now resolves the prior open relationship among:

- The Architecture of Consciousness
- Architect of Sovereignty
- The Directional Engine
- Run Your Own Code

These remain preserved as tributary sources and possible companion/workbook material rather than competing parent books.
