# Horror Short-Story Collection — Project Seed and Recovered History

## State

Incubating collection based on Edward's short-form horror-writing work.

## Recovered Original Format

- Suspenseful horror stories designed for approximately two-minute videos
- Narration-driven tension
- Text overlays used to intensify atmosphere
- Stories built for tools such as AI video generators and short-form editing platforms
- Strong emphasis on tension, mood, escalation, and final impact

## Book Potential

A collection of compact cinematic horror stories that can be read quickly but leave a lingering idea or image.

## Style Principles

- Enter late
- Establish one clear disturbance
- Escalate rapidly
- Use sensory detail economically
- End with revelation, reversal, or unresolved dread
- Avoid overexplaining the entity or threat
