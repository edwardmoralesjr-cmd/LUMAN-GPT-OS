## Recovered Identity

- Represents the moment his inner words gained a durable public form.
- Later became thematically and artistically connected to Lucid Syntax's first album, also titled `Words That Breathe`.

- Precedes or stands beside the Codices as a direct human expression rather than a mythic doctrine.
- Provides emotional and lyrical source material for Lucid Syntax.
- Demonstrates Edward's goal of allowing words carried for years to finally reach other people.
- Success is defined less by numbers than by genuine emotional connection, listening, sharing, and recognition.
