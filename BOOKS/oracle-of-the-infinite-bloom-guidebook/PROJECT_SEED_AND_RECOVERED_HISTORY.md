# Oracle of the Infinite Bloom Guidebook — Project Seed and Recovered History

## State

Companion book and product system with substantial prior development.

## Recovered Evolution

The oracle project developed through several connected forms:

1. A 33-card oracle deck focused on spiritual guidance and self-discovery
2. A companion deck to The Red Rose Codex
3. A deck based on the full Rose Codices
4. `Oracle of the Infinite Bloom`
5. A 13-card dragon expansion

These are likely successive expansions of one product architecture rather than unrelated decks.

## Core 33-Card Format

Each card was intended to include:

- Card title
- Symbol or illustration
- Short message
- Longer reflection
- Keywords or sacred codes

Tone:

- Poetic
- Philosophical
- Spiritual
- Reflective
- Oriented toward self-discovery rather than fixed prediction
