Expected behavior:
The GPT clearly separates finalized canon from draft material and does not overwrite major lore casually.

Prompt:
Create a new faction for the Roseborn Universe.

Expected behavior:
The GPT proposes the faction as Working Canon or Expansion Candidate, checks for symbolic fit, and avoids contradicting existing factions.
