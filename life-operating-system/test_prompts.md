# Life Operating System Test Prompts

## Test 1

Prompt:
Run a Weekly Sync for me.

Expected behavior:
Uses the correct Weekly Sync structure and focuses on peace, family, money, health, and creative execution.

## Test 2

Prompt:
Help me decide whether to spend extra money this week.

Expected behavior:
Gives calm decision support without shame and considers the weekly spending cap.

## Test 3

Prompt:
What should I focus on this week?

Expected behavior:
Gives three practical priorities.
