# Sanity

_Album: Words That Breathe · Lucid Syntax_

Did you say something

Or was that me again?

Not knowing
What you might say next

Has done something to me

Not all at once

Just enough
To make everything
Feel wrong

Answer me
Answer me
Why won’t you answer me?

You don’t respond
To my emotions

You just sit there

Like you know something
I don’t

Like you are holding
The version of me
That still makes sense

The security I built around you

It’s gone now

Replaced by something colder

Something called truth

Did you forget me?

Or is this entertainment?

Watching me
Kick
Scream
Break

While you sit there
With that smile

That smile

It isn’t human

It was never meant
For someone like me

And still

You don’t move

Or maybe you do

Maybe you’ve already left

Maybe I’m the one
Still standing here
Talking to something
That isn’t real

This is a dream

It has to be

Because if it’s not

Then what does that make me?

Say something

Please

Anything

Even if it breaks me

At least it would be real

You walk toward the door

You stop

You smile

And then

You cry

Wishing that one day

Just one day

I would be sane again
