# Words Unheard

_Album: Words That Breathe · Lucid Syntax_

Words go unheard.

Not because they are empty
But because they are sharp.

Sharp enough
To carve meaning
Into flesh.

Some words could help.

Some words could heal.

But the absence of sound
Leaves us tiptoeing through life,
Avoiding every door
That might lead to truth.

Not knowing.
Not caring.
Not daring
To look.

Do my words carry value?
Do my words carry value?
Or can you not accept that?

I choose every word with care.

Every pause.
Every edge.
Every weight.

Precision is not an accident.
It is the only control
I have left.

But still,
Misinterpretation enters
Like a language
No one admits they speak.

And instead of support,
I am handed silence.

Instead of compassion,
I am taught to disappear.

So I whisper destruction
Into the space
Where comfort should have been.

A quiet agony.
A quiet agony.

My purpose on this Earth
Has been revealed to me.

But what is purpose
Without witness?

What is truth
Without one ear
Willing to hold it?

I do not need the world.
I do not need applause.
I do not need every heart
To understand my cause.

I only need one ear.
One soul.
One moment
Where I am not speaking
Into nothing.

For what I have to say
Means nothing—

Except
To the ears
That care to hear.

Words go unheard.

But still…

I speak.
