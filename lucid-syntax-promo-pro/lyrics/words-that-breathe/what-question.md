# What Question

_Album: Words That Breathe · Lucid Syntax_

There is a question
It follows me
Not loudly
But constantly
From time to time
I return to it
Turning it over
Like something unfinished
What happens after death to man?
Was there ever
A plan?
The thought expands
It stretches time
It pulls me away
From where I stand
Do not dwell
On what may be
See only
What is in front of thee
Do not cloud your mind
With yesterday
If you stay here
It fades away
Stay here
Stay now
Stay where you are
Stop chasing answers
To a question
That was never meant
To be solved
Stop reaching beyond
What your hands
Can hold
Instead
Pour yourself
Into this moment
Every breath
Every step
Every fleeting second
Because once you do
The weight lifts
The noise fades
And something quiet
Takes its place
Treasures untold
Were never hidden
They were waiting
In the now
Open your eyes
Begin to thrive
Open your eyes
You are alive
For only when you live this way
Do you realize
There was never a question
