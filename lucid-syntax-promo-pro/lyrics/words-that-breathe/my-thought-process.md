# My Thought Process

_Album: Words That Breathe · Lucid Syntax_

Wait
Try to comprehend this
Not the world
Not the moment
Me
All the thoughts
That dwell within
They don’t sit still
They move
They collide
They rewrite
What I think I know
Because what surrounds us
Doesn’t just exist
It shapes
What we call now
Is this real?
Is this real?
Or am I just thinking it is?
When you reach out
Don’t hesitate
Don’t pretend
Feel it
Feel what is actually there
Not what your mind
Tells you should be
But what is real?
Are thoughts real?
Or do they own us?
Do they sit quietly?
Or do they consume
Every second we have
Until perception itself
Becomes a lie
False shapes
False meaning
False certainty
Built from something
We never questioned
Tiptoe past everything
That scares you
I try to
I fail to
I don’t even know
What lies ahead
The thoughts I choose
To entertain
They seem small
Mundane
Unimportant
To everyone else
But inside here
They are everything
So just try
Just once
Crawl inside
See it from here
But don’t mind the traffic
Signals crossing
Voices overlapping
Nothing in order
Everything loud
No silence
No filter
My thought process
Is beyond dramatic
