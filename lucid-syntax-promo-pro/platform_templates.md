# Platform Templates

## TikTok

Goal:
Short, emotionally direct, hook-driven, and comment-friendly.

Template:

```text
[Hook]

[Meaning of the song in one or two lines]

[Release detail]

[CTA]
```

## YouTube

Goal:
Cinematic, slightly longer, good for description boxes and pinned comments.

Template:

Lucid Syntax enters the [ERA] era with [SONG TITLE].

This song is about [CORE MEANING].

[Release detail]

Subscribe for more cinematic metal, lyric videos, and the Visionary rollout.

Warm, personal, human, and shareable.
