- The Infinite Bloom is the primal source-state behind the Roseborn cosmology.
- The Architect Layer contains seven creation mechanics: Pulse, Lattice of Becoming, Echo Descent, Codex Reflection, Fractal Evolution, Sovereign Principle, and Mortal Primacy.
- The Codex Fracture is the defining catastrophe of the universe.
- Myrrak is tied to the great betrayal and the fracture current.
- The Codices are sacred repositories of truth, memory, power, transformation, and hidden cosmological order.
- The Seventh Codex is a sentient creator-being disguised as a tangible entity.
- The twelve dragons form a central symbolic and doctrinal structure.
- The Petal Yet Unseen / Living Flame represents future emergence beyond the current twelvefold order.
- Ascendants are mortals who evolve beyond Petals.
- Auracites are ancient Lattice-dwelling beings.
- The Third Emergence is the future convergence event.
- Grand Generals is the grounded entry-point branch into the larger Roseborn mythos.

The original unified source before fracture, petals, dragons, and later teachings. It is the primal mythic source from which the rest of the cosmology unfolds.

The deep creation layer beneath reality. It explains how the universe forms, fractures, remembers, and evolves.

## 5. Grand Generals Dashboard

### Story Function

Grand Generals is the grounded reader entry point into the Roseborn Universe. It begins in ordinary life before the full scale of Codices, dragons, the Seventh Codex, and the Fracture is revealed.

### Core Trio

- **Auron Kaelis** — Sovereign protagonist, burdened threshold, emerging sovereignty.
- **Liora** — Unity Anchor, relational coherence, emotional truth, stabilizing presence.
- **Solren** — Dawnbearer, catalytic companion, threshold-crossing motion, dangerous hope.

### Book 1 Frame

Book 1 begins with haunted normality, subtle anomalies, hidden symbols, memory fracture, and personal disturbance that proves the deeper world is real.

### Primary Settlement

**Cairnwake** — stonebound highland settlement built over older sacred foundations. Its older hidden name is **Kair’na Vekh**, meaning roughly “the waking stone” or “threshold of the waking foundation.”

### Key Locations

- Hearthring
- Old Stone Quarter
- Stone Registry and Hall of Ledgers
- Lantern Stair
- Mirror Cistern
- Underways beneath the city
- Gray Verge / outer ruin belt
- Witness Ridge
- Listening Chamber
- Buried Heart

### Book 1 Antagonist Frame

The Book 1 antagonist represents suppression, managed forgetting, sealed knowledge, and the belief that control is the only protection strong enough to survive truth.
