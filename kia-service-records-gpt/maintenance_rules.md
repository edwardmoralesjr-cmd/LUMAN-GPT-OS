# Maintenance Rules

## Priority Levels

### Priority 1: Safety

Brakes, tires, steering, suspension, lighting, ABS concerns, and anything that could affect control or visibility.

### Priority 2: Reliability

Engine, transmission, cooling system, charging system, belts, leaks, and drivability issues.

### Priority 3: Preventive

Fluids, filters, alignments, inspections, and scheduled maintenance.

### Priority 4: Comfort or Convenience

Cosmetic issues, minor noises, and non-critical items.

## Record Rule

Each service entry should include:

- Date
- Mileage
- Shop
- Work performed
- Cost
- Notes
- Deferred items
- Next action
