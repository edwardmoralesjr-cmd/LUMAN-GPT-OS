## CONTRADICTION-001: Roseborn Series Architecture Count Conflict

### Status

Resolved at Working Canon level

### Severity

Major

### Date Logged

2026-06-18

### Date Resolved

2026-07-10

### Affected Area

Roseborn Universe master series architecture

### Versions Compared

#### Earlier 20-Book Blueprint

- Existence confirmed by later source documents
- Exact title list not recovered
- Preserved as historical reference
- Non-governing

#### Documented 23-Book Variant

- Directly listed in April 2026 Working Canon
- Five movements
- Movement II labeled Decalogy but contains twelve books
- Preserved as archived expanded variant

#### 21-Book Five-Movement Architecture

- Retained as author-approved Working Canon
- Structurally consistent with the named movement forms
- Adopted as active planning architecture

### Conflict Explanation

The 23-book master story bible states this movement pattern:

```text
Duology + Decalogy + Trilogy + Pentalogy + Finale
```

That pattern totals twenty-one books. The same file lists twelve books inside the Decalogy, producing twenty-three total books.

The two overflow titles are:

```text
The Academy of Petals
The Last Bloom Before Silence
```

### Resolution

1. Adopt the 21-book architecture as active Working Canon.
2. Preserve the 23-book version as an archived expanded variant.
3. Preserve both overflow titles as Reserved Expansion Material.
4. Preserve the 20-book blueprint as an unrecovered historical reference.
5. Do not claim the 20-book list has been reconstructed.

### Downstream Effects

- Grand Generals becomes books 16–20.
- The Third Emergence becomes book 21.
- Grand Generals titles and story content remain unchanged.
- Academy of Petals and Last Bloom Before Silence remain available but unnumbered.
