## Purpose

This template is used at the end of a Roseborn Universe writing, outlining, revision, lore, continuity, or canon-audit session.

Its purpose is to preserve what changed, what remains unresolved, what must be remembered next, and what should be carried forward into the next session.

This file supports the `/sessionupdate` command.

## Core Rule

New material remains Working Canon unless Edward explicitly promotes it to Locked Canon.

Do not promote anything to Locked Canon by assumption.

Do not smooth over contradictions.

Do not silently invent missing lore.

Every update must preserve emotional continuity, lore continuity, timeline continuity, geography continuity, and the Cost of Revelation law.
