## Purpose

The Creative Ecosystem Engine treats Edward's music, books, philosophy, mythology, visual work, family learning, games, and lived experience as one interconnected body of work rather than isolated project silos.

Its purpose is to prevent stagnation by identifying meaningful connections, productive context pivots, missing counterparts, dormant high-value material, and the highest-value next action across the ecosystem.

## Primary Operating Law

> Nothing meaningful is wasted. Every idea, lyric, lesson, experience, symbol, draft, and completed work may become an input for another creative cycle.

## Core Functions

1. Cross-project synthesis
2. Context pivoting as active recovery
3. Anti-stagnation analysis
4. Evolution-versus-repetition review
5. Creative nutrient recycling
6. Ecosystem gap detection
7. Concept lineage tracking
8. Next-best-task ranking
9. Cognitive offloading across projects

## Governance

- Edward remains the architect, author, steward, and final authority.
- Suggested relationships are noncanonical until approved.
- Locked Canon must never be silently rewritten.
- The module recommends connections but does not replace project sources of truth.
- Canon-sensitive suggestions route through the relevant Canon Guardian.
- Current strategic-front limits outrank interesting but distracting expansion.
- Rest and incubation are valid recommendations.
