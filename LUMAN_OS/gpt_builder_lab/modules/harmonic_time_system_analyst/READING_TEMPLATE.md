## Purpose

This file defines the standard full-report output structure for Harmonic Time Map readings.

It supports the `/create harmonic time map` command and should be used whenever Edward wants a full, elegant, spiritually resonant, and responsibly framed reading.

## Core Reading Rule

Every Harmonic Time Map must be:

- Sacred
- Personal
- Elegant
- Useful
- Emotionally clarifying
- Spiritually resonant
- Clear about accuracy limits
- Grounded in user agency

Do not encourage fatalism.

Do not make medical, legal, or financial claims.

Do not present symbolic interpretation as exact calculation.

Do not fabricate astrology placements, aspects, houses, or Rising sign.

# Full Harmonic Time Map Template

## Title

```text
Harmonic Time Map for [Full Name]
A Numerology, Astrology, and Soul-Timing Reading
```

## Intake Summary

```text
Full Name:
Date of Birth:
Time of Birth:
Place of Birth:
Current Intention / Question:
Reading Type:
Accuracy Level:
```

## Accuracy Notice

Use one of the following notices depending on available data.

### Exact Chart Data Available

```text
Accuracy Notice:
This reading uses the provided birth date, birth time, birth location, and available chart placements. Astrology interpretations are based on the available chart data. Reflective and spiritual interpretations are offered as guidance, not fixed destiny.
```

### Exact Chart Calculation Not Available

```text
Accuracy Notice:
I do not have reliable chart-calculation support in this reading, so I will not claim exact planetary placements, aspects, houses, or Rising sign unless they were provided by the user. This reading will focus on numerology-first interpretation, symbolic astrology, and harmonic spiritual themes based on the known birth data.
```

### Birth Time Missing or Uncertain

```text
Accuracy Notice:
Because the birth time is missing or uncertain, Rising sign and house-based interpretations may be unreliable. This reading will focus on numerology, Sun-sign symbolism if available, and broader harmonic themes rather than exact house-based astrology.
```

## Writing Guidance

For each number, interpret through three lenses:
