# Infinite Bloom Music Canonical Sound Law

## Status

Status: Active Music Canon  
Created: 2026-07-12  
Owner: Infinite Bloom Music Project

## Core Sound

```text
Dark cinematic trap metal fused with classical composition.
```

The sound must feel mythic, dangerous, intimate, and emotionally human. It should translate sacred cosmology into physical impact without becoming generic symphonic metal, conventional metalcore, or fantasy soundtrack imitation.

## Classical Foundation

Primary instruments:

- Minor-key piano
- Solo violin
- Low cellos
- Dissonant string textures
- Orchestral percussion used for weight and movement

The classical writing should function as composition, not decoration.

## Guitar Language

Every important classical phrase should be answered by expressive down-tuned guitar leads.

Core techniques:

- Harmonized bends
- Neoclassical runs
- Expressive lead phrasing
- Crushing riffs
- Dissonant accents
- Melodic counterlines
- Short solo exchanges

Violin and lead guitar should trade melodies in dramatic call-and-response. At times they harmonize. At other times they battle.

Avoid repetitive chugging as the primary identity.

## Trap and Rhythm Architecture

- Massive 808s
- Intricate trap hi-hats
- Snare rolls
- Sharp silence cuts
- Double-kick bursts
- Live-drum weight
- Rhythmic displacement
- Half-time pressure
- Sudden negative space

The trap architecture must deepen tension and movement rather than produce generic flex-oriented trap energy.

## Vocal Law

Male vocals move through:

- Intimate melodic confession
- Syncopated rap
- Cracked shouts
- Piercing high screams
- Sustained anguished screams

The transitions should feel emotionally necessary rather than included as a checklist.

## Hook Law

Hooks should collide sweeping strings with soaring guitar counter-melodies.

A hook must carry the dragon's emotional law in language a human listener can feel even without knowing the lore.

## Breakdown Law

Breakdowns remain two-stage.

Stage 1:

- Half-time 808s
- Staccato cello
- Scream callouts
- Restrained guitar pressure
- Strategic silence

Stage 2:

- Shifting guitar accents
- Double-kick displacement
- Escalating rhythmic violence
- Violin or guitar thematic return
- A transformation of the song's core motif

Breakdowns must evolve rather than repeat a static chug pattern.

## Ending Law

Default mythic ending:

```text
Violin and guitar trade one final phrase over bare piano.
```

This is a project signature, but it should be varied enough that songs do not end identically.

## Prohibitions

- No choir
- No choral vocals
- No operatic vocals
- No generic trap flexing
- No repetitive chugs
- No EDM drops
- No decorative orchestra without compositional purpose
- No flattening every dragon into the same mood
- No lore exposition that overwhelms the emotional song

## Distinct-Song Law

Every dragon song requires its own:

- Opening identity
- Tempo or pulse logic
- Classical motif
- Guitar response language
- Vocal balance
- Breakdown mutation
- Emotional center
- Ending variation

The project sound must remain recognizable while each dragon feels like a separate law of reality.
