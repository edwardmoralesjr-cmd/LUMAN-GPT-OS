# Infinite Bloom Music Project Manifest

## Status

Status: Active Building  
Created: 2026-07-12  
Owner: LUMAN OS Music Section

## Project Role

```text
The official musical vessel of the Infinite Bloom and Roseborn Universe.
```

This project translates the laws, virtues, fractures, transformations, dragons, Codices, and cosmological architecture of the Infinite Bloom into music.

It is separate from Lucid Syntax. The two projects may share Edward's authorship and mythic source material, but they must retain distinct sound identities, visual systems, release plans, and public positioning.

## Core Creative Purpose

- Give the Infinite Bloom mythos a dedicated musical body.
- Translate lore into emotionally understandable songs rather than exposition-heavy summaries.
- Let each dragon's law, virtue, distortion, wound, and transformation become a distinct human-facing musical experience.
- Build a bridge between the Roseborn novels, Codices, oracle systems, visual art, and music.

## Canonical First Album Concept

```text
A dragon-cycle album with one core song for each of the twelve Infinite Bloom dragons.
```

Possible framing tracks:

- Invocation intro
- The Petal Yet Unseen as a human-facing epilogue

The framing tracks do not replace or renumber the twelve dragon songs.

## Project Boundaries

1. Do not present the project as Lucid Syntax under a new album title.
2. Do not use Lucid Syntax's corridor, portal, red-circuit visual identity as the default.
3. Do not lock a public artist or band name until Edward approves it.
4. Do not lock a release date until album architecture and prototype songs are stable.
5. Do not invent dragon lore when a canonical source is missing. Route uncertain lore through Roseborn Canon Guardian.
6. Keep the project outside the current shipping front until Visionary's active release gates are protected.
