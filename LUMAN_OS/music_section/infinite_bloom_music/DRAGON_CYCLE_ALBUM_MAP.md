# Infinite Bloom Dragon-Cycle Album Map

## Status

Status: Working Music Canon  
Created: 2026-07-12  
Owner: Infinite Bloom Music Project

This file governs album order and music-development structure. Exact dragon doctrine remains owned by Roseborn canon sources and must be verified before lyrics are promoted to canon.

## Album Architecture

```text
Core Album: 12 dragon songs
Optional Frame: Invocation intro
Optional Epilogue: The Petal Yet Unseen
```

The invocation and epilogue are framing tracks, not additional dragons.

## Optional Framing Tracks

### Invocation Intro

Purpose:

- Open the threshold into the dragon cycle.
- Introduce piano, violin, guitar, and sub-bass as four interacting forces.
- Avoid lore dumping.
- Establish that the listener is entering a living cosmology.

### The Petal Yet Unseen

Canonical title:

```text
The Living Flame
```

Purpose:

- Translate the dragon cycle back toward the human listener.
- Function as epilogue, invitation, or unresolved continuation.
- Preserve the idea that the final living expression has not yet fully emerged.

## Required Song Identity Sheet

Before a dragon song is treated as album-ready, document:

1. Canon source basis
2. Dragon law
3. Virtue
4. Fracture or distortion
5. Human emotional lesson
6. Transformation
7. Opening identity
8. Classical motif
9. Guitar response language
10. Vocal progression
11. Two-stage breakdown mutation
12. Ending variation
13. Forbidden clichés
14. Canon risks

## Canon Safety Rule

```text
Music direction may interpret canon, but it may not silently invent canon.
```

Any uncertain doctrine must be labeled Working Interpretation and routed through Roseborn Canon Guardian.
