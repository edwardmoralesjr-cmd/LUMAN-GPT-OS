## Routing Rules

1. Use this menu for cross-project music navigation and current state.
2. Use `02_LUCID_SYNTAX/` for Lucid Syntax project truth.
3. Use `lucid-syntax-promo-pro/` for promotion-system instructions and reusable platform outputs.
4. Use `LUMAN_OS/music_section/infinite_bloom_music/` for the new Infinite Bloom musical vessel.
5. Do not merge the two projects' identities, visual language, or sound laws.
6. Do not expose unreleased masters, private distribution credentials, or private business records in the public repository.

## Music Operating Laws

- Finish active release gates before widening the shipping front.
- Preserve a distinct opening identity for every generated song.
- Avoid repetitive genre formulas, repeated intro devices, and recycled breakdown structures.
- Do not use choir elements unless Edward explicitly requests them.
- For the current Lucid Syntax writing style, avoid repeatedly using the word or image `teeth`.
- Favor intricate lyrics, intricate breakdown development, and songs that generally land around 2.5 to 3.5 minutes when the format supports it.
- Keep Lucid Syntax promotion honest without leading with AI as the primary identity.
- Default Lucid Syntax promotion platforms include TikTok, Facebook, X, YouTube Community, Pinterest, and LinkedIn.
